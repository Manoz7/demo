Food Blog

MOMO

Momo “म:म:” unanimously is the unofficial national dish of Nepal. This Nepali dumpling which comes in different shapes, sizes and with all kinds of fillings is a household name in many parts of Nepal. With simple recipes contrasting to the delectable taste, Momo is the every day food for many Nepali people. Momo is more than a dish in Nepal; it is a way of life.

Chowmein

Chow mein is one of the most popular foods in Nepal. It's a Chinese stir-fried noodle dish that's very easy to make at home and gets ready in less than 30 minutes.

TEA

Tea is a most famous drink of Nepal. To get relax and to enjoy the tasty tea remember us. Drinking tea can also boost your immune system, fight off inflammation, and even ward off cancer and heart disease.
